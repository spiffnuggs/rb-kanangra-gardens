Who We Are

We don't just build gardens.

R.B. Kanangra Gardens — formerly trading as RB Kanangra Landscapes — has been providing residential, corporate, and strata landscaping across Sydney since 1990. Founded by Ray Basha, we've built our reputation through trust, not advertising. Our clients find us through referrals from people who've watched us care for their gardens for 10, 15, even 25 years.

We're a team of 9 dedicated professionals, serving the North Shore, Eastern Suburbs, Inner West, and greater Sydney. From large corporate estates and body corporate common areas to private residential gardens, we bring the same level of horticultural expertise to every space.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How much does a landscaper cost in Sydney?

Landscaping costs in Sydney vary depending on the scope of work. R.B. Kanangra Gardens provides free on-site consultations and transparent quoting for all residential, corporate, and strata projects.

What areas of Sydney do you service?

R.B. Kanangra Gardens services all of metropolitan Sydney, including the North Shore (Mosman, Neutral Bay, Chatswood), Eastern Suburbs (Darling Point, Woollahra, Bondi), Inner West (Balmain, Leichhardt, Marrickville), Northern Beaches, Hills District, and Western Sydney. We've maintained gardens across these areas for over 35 years.

Do you handle strata and body corporate garden maintenance?

Yes — strata and body corporate garden maintenance is one of our core specialities. We maintain common areas, building entries, and apartment complex grounds across Sydney. We work closely with strata committees and building managers to deliver consistent, budget-transparent results. It was a unanimous committee decision to continue with us at multiple sites.

What does your garden maintenance service include?

Our maintenance includes lawn mowing and edging, hedge trimming and pruning, mulching and fertilising, seasonal planting, weed control, irrigation checks, garden waste removal, and garden health monitoring. Premium clients also get our Kanangra Portal with real-time visit tracking and 20-point checklists.

How long has R.B. Kanangra Gardens been operating?

R.B. Kanangra Gardens was established in 1990 by Ray Basha, making us one of Sydney's longest-running landscaping companies. Originally trading as RB Kanangra Landscapes, we have over 35 years of continuous operation and client relationships exceeding 25 years.

Can you design and build a garden from scratch?

Absolutely. R.B. Kanangra Gardens provides full garden design and landscape construction — from concept through to planting, hardscaping, retaining walls, water features, irrigation, and pool surrounds. Ray personally oversees every project and then maintains the garden long-term so it matures exactly as designed.
